Balanced Program:

Description:

The Maths Lit Program is crafted for you to be recognized and rewarded for exceptional performance in mathematical literacy.

Benefits:

Offers a comprehensive educational experience, encouraging students to excel in mathematical literacy.

How it works:

Attain a score of at least 50% in your classtests and exams (the universal pass mark).

Sustain scores above 50% to earn 1.1x more Thuto Coins.

Engage in additional support sessions like Thuto Stories to earn Thuto Coins on a daily basis . .

Achieving a 50% score activates 50% of the Thuto Coins, with the remaining 50% determined by personal performance, motivating you to excel.
