What are programs

Picture Thuto as your child's magical piggy bank for a brighter future. When you invest money into your child's Thuto account, it transforms into Thuto Coins. These coins are like rewards your child earns for doing well in their studies. Here's the cool part: Thuto is super smart. Whether your child is acing everything or putting in their best effort, they earn Thuto Coins. These coins show how they're growing and learning. Plus, any leftover coins can be saved for later or turned back into real money. So, by funding your child's Thuto account, you're giving them a fun way to learn, grow, and save for the amazing future ahead!

Types of programs

1.Universal Achievement Program

Description:
This program is like a steady compass guiding students across all subjects. It ensures a balanced focus on various subjects without bias.

Benefits:
Students gain a well-rounded academic experience, encouraging consistent performance and readiness for diverse challenges.

2. STEM-Focused Program:

Description:
Tailored for schools with a STEM focus, this program emphasises subjects like Mathematics, Science, and Technology, aligning with the school's educational objectives.

Benefits:
Encourages specialisation in STEM fields, preparing students for future careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

3. Personalized Achievement Plan:

Description:
This program is uniquely crafted for individual students, using their performance data to identify strengths and weaknesses. It's like a personal tutor, guiding them to reach specific academic goals.

Benefits:
Addresses students' unique needs, helping them excel in areas where they may struggle, and creating a path toward their desired academic and career achievements.
